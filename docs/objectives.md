# Objectives

## Core
### Demographic resistance trends
- Which age groups show the highest prevalence of resistant infections?
### Organism resistance patterns
- Which organisms have the most record of antibiotics resistance?
### Ward-based resistance analysis
- How does resistance vary across ICU, inpatient, outpatient, and ER settings?
### Antibiotic effectiveness and exposure vs resistance
- Which antibiotic classes are most associated with resistance, and does recency of prior exposure matter?
### ADI vs resistance
- Do patients from higher deprivation areas exhibit higher rates of antimicrobial resistance?
### Prior infection impact
- Does prior infection history increase the likelihood of resistant cultures?


## Supplementary
- What is the monthly trend of antibiotic resistance?
- Which antibiotics are becoming less effective against specific organisms?
- What culture types show the highest resistance burden?
